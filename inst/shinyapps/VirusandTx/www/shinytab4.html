Further Information

  • For this app, the underlying script and function running the simulation is called simulate_virus_tx.R. Read the help file for the function to see how you can call it directly.
  • For some examples of models that were used to study antivirals in acute infections, specifically influenza, see e.g. (Handel, Longini, and Antia 2007; Beauchemin et al. 2008; Canini et al. 2014).
  • For some examples of models that were applied to antiviral treatment in chronic infections (HIV, HCV), see e.g. (Neumann et al. 1998; Dixit et al. 2004).
